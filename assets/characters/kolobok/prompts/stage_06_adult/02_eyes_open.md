# stage_06_adult/eyes_open.png

Create one single isolated asset for the same adult Kolobok character.

Use the previously generated `stage_06_adult/body.png` as the visual reference for style, lighting, camera angle, and proportions. The body is a round orange blob on a pure white background. The eyes must visually match that body.

Asset name: stage_06_adult/eyes_open.png

Subject:
A pair of large cute glossy brown cartoon eyes looking forward. The eyes should fit naturally on the adult orange Kolobok body. They should feel friendly, warm, curious, and safe for children age 6-7.

Requirements:
- Eyes only.
- No orange body.
- No face shape.
- No mouth.
- No eyebrows.
- No text.
- No labels.
- No book.
- No background objects.
- Same soft 3D cartoon style as the Kolobok body.
- Same lighting direction as the body asset.
- Centered composition.
- The pair of eyes should be horizontally aligned and ready to be placed over `body.png` in Flutter.
- Keep enough empty space around the eyes so they can be cropped and positioned.
- Use pure white background only.
- Do not use black background.
- Do not add shadows under the eyes.
- Do not draw any orange shape behind the eyes.
- High contrast edges, easy to remove background.
- Square image, 1024x1024.

Important:
The result must be only the eyes layer. Do not draw the orange character body again.

Composition guide:
- Put the eye pair in the center of the image.
- The full eye pair should occupy about 45-55% of the image width.
- Leave clean white space around the eyes.

Result:
- Saved as `assets/characters/kolobok/stage_06_adult/eyes_open.png`.
- Size: 1024x1024.
- White background. Good enough for the current workflow; background will be removed later.
